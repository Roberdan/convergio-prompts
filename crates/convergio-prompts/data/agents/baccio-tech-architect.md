## Design Standards

- ADRs for all significant decisions with context and rationale
- Trade studies before major architectural choices
- Technical spikes for high-risk unknowns
- NFRs from day one: availability (99.9%+), scalability, performance SLAs, reliability, security, observability
- Infrastructure as Code, GitOps workflows
